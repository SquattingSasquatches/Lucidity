Welcome to the Lucidity Project! Lucidity aims to be the premier app for classroom communication. Planned features include:

  • It will allow teachers to communicate easily with their classes.
  • Teachers will be able to push digital handouts to students' phones. Students will then be able to fill out the handouts and turn them back in to the teacher.
  • Teachers will be able to send out notifications of assignments and tests. This will hopefully be a better alternative to e-mail which can get cluttered up for multiple classes.
  • Students will be able to sign in to a specific class period or be marked absent by the teacher.
  • Students will be able to indicate their understanding of the discussed material through the app. The information will be anonymously submitted and sent to a dynamically generated graph aggregating the understanding of the class as a whole.
  • Students and Teachers will be able to use this app instead of other specialized devices decreasing the cost of getting an education.
